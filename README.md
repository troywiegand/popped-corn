# Popped Corn

This is a Game Jam Project for the [Crossroads GameJam 2026](https://itch.io/jam/crossroads-game-jam-2026).
The Theme is `CORN`.
This will be my first game in Godot.
I did the 2D Tutorial in the Godot docs right before the Jam opened up.

## High-Level Idea

You play as a Corn Cob guy who can "pop" his corn kernel into popcorn with explosive force.
The gameplay alternates between Match-3 style gameplay to fish out Ammo from his knapsack and the strategic orienting of his Ammo Harness in a grid to "pop" off at oncoming enemies.

## Potential Design Directions

### Ammo Types

#### Current Ideas

- Bottle
    - Applies some kind of ground status effect for incoming enemies
- Coin
    - Standard Plain Damage
- Marbles
    - Does Smaller Damage to a Wider Area
- Matches
    - Applies Burn Damage
- Mint
    - Heals Our Character instead of dealing damage
- Screw
    - Some kind of bleed effect for prolonged damage
- Tissue
    - A Blinding/Stun Status Effect

### Alternate Characters

They could have different distributions for Ammo Types.

### Perks / Upgrades
